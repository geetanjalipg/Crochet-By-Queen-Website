Crochet Queen

Hi I am a Crochet queen!! I like making things out of crochet. For example scrunchies,hairband, Rubber band and headbands. I post my progress on social media. I like doing a Crochet in my free time. I try making new things every day and discover new techniques and knots.

About My Work
When I do a Crochet I feel calm and go in to a relax mindset.When I start a project I first go through all my steps and gather my materials. Then I start working on it and take breaks between so that it is easier and not very tiring. When I make a mistake I do not let it put me down. I do it again it and makes me feel calmer and better. When I am energetic I finish the project in one hour or so. When I am a little tired I take breaks or let it be overnight. If I do my work when I am tired it does not come out the best. When I take a break it relaxes my mind and give me better ideas.
